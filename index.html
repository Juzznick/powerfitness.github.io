About Us

Power Fitness, the premier gym in Thrissur, is the go-to center for health enthusiasts. Our fully equipped facility caters to both men and women, helping them achieve their fitness goals. Join us today and experience transformation in a celebratory environment. Be a part of our fitness family!.
